Multiline Formulae

{\tt\string\eqalign}$\{\}$ or {\tt\string\eqalignno}$\{\}$
The later produce equation number on each line for which you insert
`$\&equation\ number$' immediately before the {\tt\string\cr}

$$ \eqalign{\cos 2\theta &= \cos^2\theta - \sin^2\theta \cr
			 &= 2\cos^\theta - 1 \cr} $$

$$ \eqalignno{\sin 2\theta &= 2\sin\theta \cos\theta	&(6)\cr
     \cos 2\theta   &= \cos^2\theta - \sin^2\theta \cr
		    &= 2\cos^2\theta - 1	&(7)\cr} $$

cases
$$ |x| = \cases{ 
	    x	&if $x \geq 0$ \cr
	    -x  &if $x < 0$ \cr
	    }$$
